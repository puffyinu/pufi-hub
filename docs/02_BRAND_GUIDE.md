# PUFI HUB Branding Guide

## Brand Overview

PUFI HUB is the premier decentralized advertising marketplace within the World ecosystem. It combines the security of World ID with the transparency of World Chain to create a trustless bridge between creators and verified humans. The brand is built on the pillars of authenticity, transparency, and human-centric rewards.

## Mission

To build a production-ready, decentralized advertising platform where every interaction is verified by World ID, ensuring advertisers reach real humans and users receive fair rewards for their attention.

## Vision

To become the foundational advertising layer for the World ecosystem, providing a seamless bridge between creators and verified humans through a trustless, transparent, and rewarding marketplace.

## Brand Identity

The PUFI HUB identity is defined by its focus on **Human Verification**. This is represented through high-impact visual indicators, emerald green status badges, and a friendly yet professional aesthetic that balances blockchain technology with human accessibility.

## Brand Values

- **Authenticity:** Guaranteed by World ID and Proof of Personhood.
- **Transparency:** All rewards and transactions are handled by smart contracts on World Chain.
- **Human-Centric:** Prioritizing the user's attention as a valuable asset.
- **Professionalism:** High-performance architecture and production-ready implementation.

## Logo Usage

### Wordmark
The primary logo is the **PUFI HUB** wordmark.
- **Weight:** Black / Heavy.
- **Tracking:** Tight for headings, wide for labels.
- **Gradient:** `from-white to-slate-400` (Vertical).

### Assets
- `public/images/brand/pufi-logo.png`: Primary brand mark.
- `public/images/brand/pufi-coin.png`: Representation of the ecosystem token.
- `public/images/brand/logo-coin-mascot.png`: Integrated brand asset.

## Color Palette

### Primary Backgrounds
- **Base:** `#0F172A` (Slate 900) - Used for the core application shell.
- **Premium Gradient:**
  - Start: `#2A1757` (Deep Purple)
  - Middle: `#181633` (Midnight Blue)
  - End: `#0D1125` (Deep Navy)

### Accent Colors
- **World Blue:** `#2563EB` (Blue 600) - Used for primary actions and World Wallet connectivity.
- **Gold Accent:** `#FFC857` / `#FACC15` (Yellow 400) - Used for connectivity status and premium glows.
- **Emerald Green:** `#10B981` (Emerald 500) - Used exclusively for "Human" and "Verified" status indicators.

### Component Styling
- **Card Background:** `#1E2A4A` / `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)`
- **Card Border:** `#31456E` / `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`
- **Glassmorphism:** `backdrop-blur-2xl` for overlay components.

## Typography

### Primary Typeface
- **Font:** Geist Sans (Standardized Next.js font).
- **Usage:** Headings, UI labels, and primary body text.
- **Characteristics:** Geometric, modern, highly legible on mobile displays.

### Monospace Typeface
- **Font:** Geist Mono.
- **Usage:** Technical readouts, wallet addresses, and system statuses.

### Text Styles
- **Headings:** Black (900) weight, tracking-tight.
- **Labels:** Bold, uppercase, wide tracking (`tracking-widest`).
- **Secondary:** Slate-400/500 for descriptive text and sub-headers.

## Iconography

PUFI HUB utilizes a mix of standard emojis and custom graphic assets to maintain a "Mini App" feel that is consistent with the World App environment.
- **Status Icons:** 🟢 (Verified), 👛 (Wallet), 📅 (Events), 🎁 (Rewards).
- **Official Icons:** World App, MiniKit, and World Chain marks used in footers and gateway screens.

## Mascot

### PUFI
PUFI is the official mascot of the ecosystem, representing a friendly and approachable gateway to decentralized advertising.
- **Asset:** `public/images/mascot/pufi-mascot.png`.
- **Personality:** Helpful, playful, and observant.

### Visual Effects
- **Floating:** Vertical oscillation to suggest "airiness" and lightness.
- **Swaying:** Gentle rotation to add life to static screens.
- **Glow:** Blue-themed pulse to indicate "Human Verification" energy.
- **Shine:** Periodic gleam effect to represent premium quality and "Proof of Personhood" luster.

## Visual Style

PUFI HUB employs a **Premium Dark Mode** aesthetic:
- **Depth:** Created through multi-layered gradients and aurora-style blurs (Purple and Gold glows).
- **Softness:** Large border-radii (`rounded-[24px]` to `rounded-[28px]`) for cards and buttons.
- **Feedback:** Interactive elements use scale transitions (`active:scale-[0.98]`) and opacity shifts.

## UI Design Language

- **Mobile-First:** Standard viewport max-width of `480px`.
- **Safe Areas:** Strict adherence to device safe areas using `env(safe-area-inset-*)`.
- **Navigation:** Persistent Bottom Navigation for high-frequency interactions.
- **Information Hierarchy:** Dashboard as the central hub, with actions decentralized to specific modules.

## Naming Convention

- **Product:** PUFI HUB (Always uppercase wordmark).
- **Company:** PUFFY INU.
- **Creators:** Advertisers/Apps are referred to as "Creators".
- **Tasks:** Actions within a campaign are "Achievements".
- **Rewards:** Referred to as "Claims".

## Token Branding

- **PUFI:** The primary reward token. Displayed with the `pufi-coin.png` asset.
- **WLD:** Worldcoin, used for ecosystem context.
- **USDC:** The stable currency for campaign funding in the Creator Center.

## Creator Branding Rules

- **Creator Center:** Must feel administrative yet accessible.
- **Themes:** Blue and Slate theme to distinguish from the user-facing Dashboard.
- **Components:** Task lists and creation flows utilize clean, bordered containers.

## Campaign Branding Rules

- **Human Verification:** Every campaign must display the "Human Verified" status.
- **Urgency:** Active campaigns use "READY TO EARN" banners.
- **Completion:** Completed states transition to Emerald Green ("DONE").

## Documentation Branding

All technical and architectural documentation must follow the structured PUFI HUB documentation standards (e.g., `00_PROJECT_OVERVIEW.md`), utilizing clear headings, lists, and technical justifications.

## Future Branding Guidelines

As PUFI HUB evolves, the branding will expand to include:
- **Social Branding:** Community-driven assets and social reward visuals.
- **Governance:** Specialized branding for PUFI token governance modules.
- **Expansion:** Cross-chain branding variants while maintaining the core "World-First" identity.

## Conclusion

The PUFI HUB brand is a commitment to a human-verified future. By maintaining consistency across colors, typography, and mascot usage, we ensure a trusted and recognizable experience for every user and creator in the World ecosystem.
